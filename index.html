🏫 About Our School

A Legacy of Learning

Govt. G.P. High School, Kusanga is a government-recognized secondary school under the School & Mass Education Department, Govt. of Odisha, committed to quality education for rural students of Boudh district.

🎓

Quality Education

BSE Odisha curriculum for Classes 9 & 10 with qualified and dedicated teaching staff.

🌱

Holistic Development

Emphasis on academics, sports, cultural activities, and character building for every student.

🤝

Inclusive Learning

Open to all students regardless of caste or economic background. Scholarships available.

🏅

Govt. Recognized

Fully recognized by Govt. of Odisha and affiliated to Board of Secondary Education (BSE), Odisha.
